Values

Tube Fab/Roman Engineering implores the following set of values for the business:

  • Quality - "Pursuing excellence in everything we do."
  • Integrity - "Doing what we say and treating others as we wish to be treated."
  • Innovation - "Passionate about developing better methods for tomorrow."
  • Service - "Daily earning the trust of our customers, co-workers and our community."

Vision

Tube Fab/Roman Engineering vision for the business:

  • "Building a better future for our people, and our community through innovation, learning and trust."

Mission

Tube Fab/Roman Engineering mission for the business:

  • "To provide an outstanding experience for our customers by empowering employees to make meaningful contributions in a safe and rewarding environment."
